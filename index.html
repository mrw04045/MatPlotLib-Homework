MatPlotLib Homework--Pymaceuticals Inc

Pymaceuticals Inc. specializes in drug-based, anti-cancer pharmaceuticals.

In their most recent efforts, they've since begun screening for potential treatments to squamous cell carcinoma (SCC), a commonly occurring form of skin cancer.

The objective in this project was to analyze the data to show how four treatments (Capomulin, Infubinol, Ketapril, and Placebo) compare.
